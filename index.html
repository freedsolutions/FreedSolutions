The Problem
Bad data quietly eats your business

When you're running a cannabis business, bad data quietly eats your time, your margins, and your peace of mind. Spreadsheets don't match. Systems don't talk. Every "simple" question gets unnecessarily complicated due to METRC issues, shifting regulations, and an overly complicated tech stack.

Legal markets were supposed to make cannabis clearer and safer. Instead, many operators are still trapped in a fog of half-matching reports, siloed spreadsheets, and dashboards that don't agree.

I help founders and operators get out of that chaos so they can run calmer, smarter, more resilient businesses.
